PREPOSITIONS OF TIME AND PLACE
PREPOSITIONS OF TIME
AT
AT + Specific Time
I get up at 7 o'clock. Lunch is at midday.
We meet at noon.
AT
AT + Holiday Period
They sing carols at Christmas.
The family meets at Easter time.
ON
ON + Days and Dates
I will visit you on Wednesday.
His birthday is on March 27th. The exam is on the 16th.
IN
IN + Months and Years
My birthday is in January. Holiday in August.
Shakespeare born in 1564. Titanic sank in 1912.
IN
IN + Decade and Century
Life was difficult in the 1940s.
We live in the 21st century.
Built in the sixth century.
IN
IN + Season
We go to the beach in summer.
Flowers in spring.
AT/IN
AT night - IN morning / afternoon / evening
She works at night. We exercise in the morning.
Lunch in the afternoon. Dinner in the evening.
PREPOSITIONS OF PLACE
AT
AT + Specific Point or Location
His office is at 23 Pine Street.
She is waiting at the entrance. He is at the gym.
ON
ON + Surface
The book is on the table. The poster is on the wall.
We live on the third floor.
IN
IN + Enclosed Space or Building
The car is in the garage.
Children are in the classroom.
IN
IN + Large Area (City, Country, Region)
Jack is in the tent. We live in Paris.
Opera House is in Australia. Jack is in the Amazon.
Days and Dates - ON | Months, Seasons, Years - IN | Times and Holidays - AT
Jack hides IN a cave, ON a cliff, AT midnight!
HOW TO PLAY
¿Cómo jugar?
📋 Instrucciones
  1. Lee la frase completa en la pantalla.
  2. Encuentra el hueco ___ en la frase.
  3. Toca el cofre correcto: AT, IN o ON.
  4. La preposición correcta aparecerá automáticamente.
🎯 Las preposiciones
  • 🟠 AT → horas exactas y momentos específicos
    at 7 o'clock, at night, at Christmas
  • 🟢 IN → meses, estaciones, años y períodos
    in July, in summer, in 1987, in the morning
  • 🔵 ON → días de la semana y fechas
    on Monday, on the 25th of September
✅ Correcto: ¡Fuegos artificiales! Siguiente frase.
❌ Incorrecto: Pantalla roja. ¡Inténtalo de nuevo!
