📖 HOW TO PLAY
¿Cómo jugar?
📋 Instrucciones
  1. Lee la frase completa en la pantalla.
  2. Encuentra el hueco ___ en la frase.
  3. Toca el cofre del tesoro correcto: AT, IN o ON.
  4. La preposición correcta aparecerá automáticamente en el hueco.
🎯 Las preposiciones
  • 🟠 AT → horas exactas y momentos específicos
    at 7 o'clock, at night, at Christmas
  • 🟢 IN → meses, estaciones, años y períodos
    in July, in summer, in 1987, in the morning
  • 🔵 ON → días de la semana y fechas
    on Monday, on the 25th of September
✅ Correcto: ¡Fuegos artificiales! Pasa a la siguiente frase.
❌ Incorrecto: Pantalla roja. ¡Inténtalo de nuevo!

怎么玩？
📋 游戏说明
  1. 仔细阅读屏幕上的完整句子。
  2. 找到句子中的空格 ___。
  3. 点击正确的宝箱：AT、IN 或 ON。
  4. 正确的介词会自动填入空格中。
🎯 介词用法
  • 🟠 AT → 具体时间或特定时刻
    at 7 o'clock 七点，at night 夜晚，at Christmas 圣诞节
  • 🟢 IN → 月份、季节、年份或时间段
    in July 七月，in summer 夏天，in 1987 一九八七年，in the morning 早上
  • 🔵 ON → 星期几或具体日期
    on Monday 周一，on the 25th of September 九月二十五日

❌ 答错了： 屏幕会闪烁红光。请再试一次！

🎯 Предлоги

    on Monday — в понедельник, on the 25th of September — 25 сентября

कैसे खेलें?
📋 निर्देश
  1. स्क्रीन पर पूरा वाक्य ध्यान से पढ़ें।
  2. वाक्य में खाली जगह ___ ढूंढें।
  3. सही खज़ाने के संदूक को छुएँ: AT, IN या ON।
  4. सही प्रीपोज़िशन अपने आप खाली जगह में भर जाएगा।
🎯 प्रीपोज़िशन का उपयोग
  • 🟠 AT → सटीक समय और खास पल
    at 7 o'clock — 7 बजे, at night — रात में, at Christmas — क्रिसमस पर
  • 🟢 IN → महीने, मौसम, साल और समय की अवधि
    in July — जुलाई में, in summer — गर्मियों में, in 1987 — 1987 में, in the morning — सुबह
  • 🔵 ON → सप्ताह के दिन और तारीख़ें
    on Monday — सोमवार को, on the 25th of September — 25 सितंबर को
✅ सही जवाब: आतिशबाज़ी दिखाई देगी! अगले वाक्य पर जाएँ।
❌ गलत जवाब: स्क्रीन लाल रंग में चमकेगी। फिर से कोशिश करें!
किसी भी समय 📋 बटन दबाकर व्याकरण की तालिका देखें।

PREPOSITIONS OF TIME & PLACE
⏰ PREPOSITIONS OF TIME
AT
AT + Specific Time
I get up at 7 o'clock.
Lunch is at midday. We meet at noon.
AT
AT + Holiday Period
They sing carols at Christmas.
The family meets at Easter time.
ON
ON + Days & Dates
I will visit you on Wednesday.
His birthday is on March 27th.
The exam is on the 16th.
IN
IN + Months & Years
My birthday is in January.
I'm going on holiday in August.
Shakespeare was born in 1564.
The Titanic sank in 1912.
IN
IN + Decade / Century
Life was difficult in the 1940s.
We are living in the 21st century.
The building was built in the sixth century.
IN
IN + Season
We go to the beach in summer.
There are many flowers in spring.
AT/IN
AT night · IN the morning/afternoon/evening
She works at night.
We exercise in the morning.
Lunch is in the afternoon.
Dinner is in the evening.
📍 PREPOSITIONS OF PLACE
AT
AT + Specific Point / Location
His office is at 23 Pine Street.
She is waiting at the main entrance.
He is at the gym.
ON
ON + Surface (horizontal or vertical)
The book is on the table.
The poster is on the wall.
We live on the third floor.
IN
IN + Enclosed Container / Building
The car is in the garage.
The children are in the classroom.
IN
IN + Large Area (City, Country, Region)
Jack is in the hut.
We live in Paris.
The Sydney Opera House is in Australia.
Jack is in the Amazon rainforest.
💡 Days & Dates → ON  |  Months, Seasons, Years, Enclosed spaces → IN  |  Times, Holidays, Specific points → AT
