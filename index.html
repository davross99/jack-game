PREPOSITIONS OF TIME
AT
AT + Specific Time
AT + Holiday Period
Jack wakes up at 6 o'clock.
The plane leaves at 8.30.
They meet at noon / at midnight.
Jack arrives at night / at sunset.
Schools close at Christmas.
"Meet me at the gate at ten o'clock!"
ON
ON + Days of the Week
ON + Dates
Jack sets off on Monday.
The guide arrives on Friday.
They rest on Sunday.
The festival is on the 25th of September.
"We celebrate on Christmas Day!"
IN
IN + Morning / Afternoon / Evening
IN + Months · Seasons · Years · Decades
Jack leaves in the morning.
They travel in the afternoon.
Stars appear in the evening.
Peru is hot in July / in summer.
"It rains in September," says the chief.
The temple was built in the 6th century.
Explorers came in the 1920s.
"I will be back in three hours."
